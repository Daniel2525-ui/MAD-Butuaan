About MAD Butuan

Founded by Sam Cabrera, Midnight Automotive Deals (MAD Butuan) began with a passion for cars, precise installations, and quality automotive upgrades. What started as a hobby has grown into a trusted name in Butuan City, known for high-quality, affordable installs done right the first time. At MAD Butuan, we specialize in expert installation of automotive lighting like LED headlights, fog lamps, and taillights, along with premium tires and carefully selected car accessories. We don’t just add parts — we focus on precision, alignment, and a clean, factory-like finish that makes every upgrade look original to your vehicle. Our services go beyond installations to include engine troubleshooting, transmission repairs, electrical system diagnostics, suspension and steering work, heating and air conditioning servicing, and wheel repair and replacement. We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you drive every day, enjoy weekend cruising, or show off a custom ride, we treat every project with care and expertise. We make sure to explain everything clearly so you feel confident with each service and upgrade. Proudly serving Butuan City and nearby areas, MAD Butuan delivers top-tier service with a personal touch — even during after-hours. That’s why we’re called “Midnight Automotive Deals.” Day or night, we’re here to help transform your vehicle into something you’re proud to drive. Trust MAD Butuan for sharp installs, smart recommendations, and results you can count on.
